Open Day 2021

Friday , 23rd April 2021, 9.00 am - 1.00pm, Virtual Event (Microsoft Teams)

The Microsoft Africa Development Center invites you to attend our inaugural Open Day. This is a fully virtual event that will bring together industry professionals and students under the theme 'Matching technology skills to job market needs' . Join us to learn more about the ADC and the opportunities available in the software engineering industry.


During this meetup, we'll have Prof. Bitange Ndemo, Professor of Entrepreneurship - UON and Mr. Kamau Gachigi, Executive Director - Gearbox as our guest speakers. Join us for an engaging conversation on how to continuously learn new trends & skills to keep up with market needs.

Microsoft ADC

The Africa Development Center (ADC) represents Microsoft's first-ever engineering offices in Africa. With locations in both Nairobi, Kenya and Lagos, Nigeria, the ADC is a premier center of Engineering for Microsoft.
